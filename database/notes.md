# Updated Contact/Lead Stages with Scheduled

## 1. New / Received
- Just submitted, no action yet.  
- Status examples: `"new"`, `"received"`, `"pending"`

## 2. Reviewed / Assigned
- Someone has seen it and assigned it.  
- Status examples: `"reviewed"`, `"assigned"`

## 3. Contacted / In Progress
- You reached out and may be waiting for a response.  
- Status examples: `"contacted"`, `"in_progress"`

## 4. Scheduled / Booked
- The lead has agreed to a time for service, a demo, or an appointment.  
- Status examples: `"scheduled"`, `"booked"`  
- Optional: `"confirmed"` if you want to track confirmation separately.

## 5. Follow-up / Waiting
- Waiting for the lead to respond or take next action.  
- Status examples: `"follow_up"`, `"awaiting_response"`

## 6. Closed / Completed
- Interaction is finished. Either converted to a client or not.  
- Status examples: `"closed"`, `"completed"`, `"converted"`, `"rejected"`
